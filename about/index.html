ABOUT

PLUS ONE is a family-owned and operated coffee roasting company, located in Eastern Iowa.  You can reach us at 319-683-3357, or by email. 

All of our roasting is done in our house on the kitchen stove.  We roast in very small batches, so that all of our product is as carefully crafted as possible.
